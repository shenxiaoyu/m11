PAST EXPERIENCE

My former education experiences supported me with practical design skills in Graphic design, User Interface design, Product design and so on. During my bachelor education, I focused more on the deliverables themselves rather than my own development as a designer. Although I did improve my skills of design to certain degree, I was proceeding aimlessly on the road towards a qualified designer. As a consequence, when I came to the the department of industrial design of TU/e, my weakness were all exposed to this new education system with requirement on rounded skills. At the beginning of the semester, I made a Personal Development Plan to re-organize my development as a designer. As I would conduct a research project, I will focus on the competency area of Design and Research Processes(DRP). In the meanwhile, in order to do a user-centered design research, Social-cultural Awareness(SCA) and User Focus and Perspective(UFP) will also be the main skills to be developed in the project.

From another perspective, it was my first experience of study abroad. Language, cultural differences, living individually were challenges for me. I decided to take the first year as a adaption semester to get familiar with the new education system as well as the new foreign environment.

NOW: ACTIVITIES IN THIS SEMESTER

As summarized in the section of “Work”, I have got considerable progress in my targeted skills. In this section, I will give brief conclusions for my main growth in this semester.

 

Design Research and Process

Based on my past experience, I was a designer with limited research skills. In this semester, I focused on the competency area of Design Research and Process and learned both the research skills and thinking. In the project, near ten methods were adopted to gain a better understanding of how to deal with a research. Instead of learning by reading books, this project provided me with the opportunity to practice. Except for the specific skills, research thinking is even more valuable. It is a habit of questioning, a systematic way of examining to provide emotional and seneitive design with logical and rational thinking. And I will continue with it and start a design project in the next stage.

 

User Focus and Perspective

Combined with method of multi-perspective observation I learned from the module, Intercultural Markers in Design Process, I developed my skills on user-centered research project. In order to get a closer approach to users, observation, interview and several methods of analysis were adopted. For example, one of my improvement is how to communicate with people in user-centered exploration. I found it skillful to ask questions, the words need to lead a direction and at the same time avoid strong suggestions. Only in this way, the respond could be natural and reliable.

 

Communication

As it is the very first year I study abroad, I took it as an adaption semester to get familiar with everything. One of the big challenge for me is communication. From the feedbacks of first two modules, I was always given suggestions like being more active and making yourself heard. I was used to sit in the background and listen to people at beginning, but I practiced a lot to make progress in this semester. In the last module, the lecturer was kind of satisfied with my ability on communication. And I was more confidenced in myself to state my opinion in front of people.
