VISION EXPLORATION

Two years ago I made my mind to change my major from Medicine to Industrial Design. For me, design is a surprising and unpredictable adventure which can provide human beings with a better world to live in. During my bachelor education, I  showed an enthusiasm on all kinds of design areas, but broad interests made it difficult to dive into a specific area. As a result, my thoughts on design is still in the stage of exploration. In this semester, I will put more emphasis on what kind of designer I want to be and develop my own vision of design through practice.

VISION OF DESIGN

- Intelligent delight -

IDENTITY

- Interaction designer -

Nowadays, the overwhelming numbers of new things were fulfilling our lives, high-technology, new media and so on. A revolution is brewing for a brand-new intelligent society. In this new era of industrial design, focus is transferring from pure visual enjoyment or functionality to the combination of technology and traditional craftsmanship.

From another perspective, people are main stakeholders in the design process. Except for aesthetic and functional products, user experiences become more and more crucial for a good design now. In my point of view, the definition of good design is quite simple: something that is able to create pleasure for people. Based on this, I expect to use digital products as tools to create intelligent satisfaction and delight.

 

In this semester, I conducted a research project which required a close approach to users. Through that, I found my interests in User-centered design. At current phase, I was aiming to be an interaction designer with enthusiasm towards the thoughts and behavior of human beings. Design is an emotional word, like literature and art, people are the center of this subject. From the perspective of process, design should not focus on the result only, but a lasting enjoyable experience. How to translate the functionality realization into a playful and intuitive solution is what I want to discuss with my design. It requires me, as a designer, to gain empathy with users and have a better understanding of their emotion process.

APPROACH

-T-shaped Individual-

In order to equip myself with rounded knowledge and skills, I aim to be a T-shaped individual, which is presented as a standard of employment in IDEO. The height of T indicates the depth of expertise I have in design. The cross on the top indicates the breadth of knowledge or set of skills I have cross disciplines, such as cultural recognition, technology and business senses. Culture helps us to understand the behavior and thoughts of users. Technologies bring new possibilities to interaction design, especially in the combination with physical objects. Business, on the other hand, brings a chance to test design in a real-life societal context.

As for this semester, I will start with a research project, which requires a depth in research skills. In addition, Socio-cultural Awareness and User Focus and Perspective would be two important competency areas to develop which connects tightly to the theme of the project, Worn Identity.
